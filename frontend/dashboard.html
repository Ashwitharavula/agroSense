Quick Insights

Soil Health Alert

Maintaining a soil pH between 6.0 and 7.0 is ideal for most cereal crops like Rice and Wheat.

Nitrogen Optimization

High nitrogen levels encourage leaf growth, whereas phosphorus is critical for root development.

Crop Rotation Tip

Rotate grains with legume crops (beans/peas) to naturally restore soil nitrogen levels.
